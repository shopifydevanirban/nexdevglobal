FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before we start working together. Still have questions? Just reach out!

Ask Us Directly →
How long does it take to build a Shopify store?
+

A basic store takes 3–5 days. A custom-designed store typically takes 7–14 days depending on the number of pages, features, and revisions. We'll give you a clear timeline before we begin.

Do you build dropshipping stores?
+

Yes! We specialize in dropshipping stores using DSers, AutoDS, and CJ Dropshipping. We'll handle product research, supplier connection, automated fulfillment, and the full store setup.

Can you manage my Amazon seller account too?
+

Absolutely. We offer full Amazon Seller Central management — from listing creation and A+ content to PPC advertising, brand registry, and ongoing account optimization.

What payment methods do you accept?
+

We accept payment via Fiverr, PayPal, Wise (TransferWise), and bank transfer. We typically work with a 50% upfront deposit and 50% on delivery for direct clients.

Do you offer post-launch support?
+

Yes! All projects come with post-launch support — 30 days on Professional package, 60 days on Agency. We're also available for ongoing retainer-based maintenance and growth work.

Can you redesign my existing Shopify store?
+

Yes, store redesigns are one of our most common requests. We can refresh your design, improve speed, restructure your navigation, and fix conversion leaks — all while keeping your existing data and products intact.
